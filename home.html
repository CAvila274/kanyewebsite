Basic info about Kanye

Kanye West is a highly influential and multi-talented American artist who has made significant contributions to the music industry, fashion, and popular culture. Born on June 8, 1977, in Atlanta, Georgia, he rose to prominence as a rapper, but his impact extends far beyond just music.
